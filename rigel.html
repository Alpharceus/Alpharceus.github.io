Rigel — Quantum Playground

A quick tour of the Bloch sphere, then a toy quantum circuit simulator you can play with.

Drag the sphere to rotate it. Pure states live on the surface; the center is maximally mixed.

The arrow is the live (reduced) state of the selected qubit from the circuit below — entangle it and watch the arrow shrink toward the center.
