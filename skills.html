Skills — a photonic routing tree

One beam of light enters on the left and meets a binary tree of 31 Mach–Zehnder interferometers — the same tunable building block as the electrically tuned MZI networks I work on. Each interferometer's phase shifter sets how much light continues up or down, and the 32 output ports are my skills.
